Carole Gray (born 1940 in Bulawayo, Rhodesia (today's Zimbabwe) is a former British dancer and actress who in the 1960s, particularly in West End musicals. She appeared in television series such as "The Avengers", and made her film debut as Cliff Richard's girlfriend Toni in "The Young Ones" (1961). Thereafter, she appeared mainly in horror films, such as "Curse of the Fly", "Devils of Darkness", and "Island of Terror", and was given the nickname the "Scream Queen". She was married to diamond heir Douglas Cullinan.
