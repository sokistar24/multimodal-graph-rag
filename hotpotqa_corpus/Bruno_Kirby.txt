Bruno Kirby (born Giovanni Quidaciolu, Jr.; April 28, 1949 – August 14, 2006) was an American actor, singer, voice artist, chef, and comedian. He was known for his roles in "City Slickers", "When Harry Met Sally...", "Good Morning, Vietnam", "The Godfather Part II", and "Donnie Brasco". He voiced Reginald Stout in "Stuart Little".
