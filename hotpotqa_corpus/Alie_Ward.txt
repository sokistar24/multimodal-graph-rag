Alison Ann Ward is a writer, a painter, American TV actress and co-host of the 2013 Cooking Channel travel-food television series, "Tripping Out with Alie & Georgia". She is also a correspondent for the CBS series "Innovation Nation". Ward has written for L.A. Weekly and the "Los Angeles Times". She has appeared in a handful of TV shows, including "Nash Bridges". She achieved notoriety from her "Alie and Georgia" online videos and television appearances, where she and her close friend, Georgia Hardstark, offer up their recipes for some rather odd culinary concoctions, such as the infamous McNuggetini.
