Love Beats Rhymes (working title "Coco") is an upcoming American musical drama film directed by RZA. The film stars Azealia Banks as a twenty five-year-old female rapper from Brooklyn, New York who wants to pursue a music career. The film also stars Lorraine Toussaint, Jill Scott, Lucien Laviscount, MC Jin, Hana Mae Lee, and Common. The film is awaiting release by CodeBlack Films and Lionsgate.
