"Outside" is a song by Scottish DJ and record producer Calvin Harris from his fourth studio album, "Motion" (2014). The song features English singer Ellie Goulding and was released on 20 October 2014 as the fourth single from the album. It marks the second collaboration between Harris and Goulding, following the single "I Need Your Love" (2013). "Outside" is also included on the deluxe edition of Goulding's third studio album, "Delirium".
