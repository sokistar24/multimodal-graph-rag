Lanre Dabiri (born May 23, 1977), better known by his stage name Eldee, stylized as eLDee, is a former Nigerian-American rapper, singer, and record producer but now an IT Consultant based in the United_States "Lanre" is a diminutive for the Yoruba name "Olanrewaju" (which translates to ""Wealth is moving forward""). eLDee has a masters degree in Architecture from the University of Lagos, Nigeria. He is an original member of the band Trybesmen, which was started in 1998 with rappers KB and Freestyle. He hails from Lagos Island in Lagos State of Nigeria.
