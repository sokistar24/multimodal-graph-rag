Snackbox Food Holdings Co, Ltd (Korean: 스낵박스푸드홀딩스 ) is a Korean lunch snack food company. Its headquarters is in Mokdong-Ri Gyoha-Eup Paju Gyeonggi-do, Korea, and was established in 2004. It offers a number of snack food services to home party foods. The company offers a range of products including fruit lunch packs, salad, yogurt and sandwiches. Snack Box has about 25 employees as of January, 2006.
