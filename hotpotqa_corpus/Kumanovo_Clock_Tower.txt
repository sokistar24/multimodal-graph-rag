Kumanovo Clock Tower (Macedonian: Саат Кула Куманово ) was a clock tower in Kumanovo, Ottoman Empire (today Republic of Macedonia). The tower is believed to have existed since the second half of the 18th century but there are now known historical facts. It was near Eski Mosque in the former Orta Bunar Neighborhood (Macedonian: Орта Бунар Маало ). It was demolished after the Second World War.
