Rajneesh Duggal is an Indian film actor and a former model. He is the winner of Grasim Mr. India title in 2003 and a runner up in Mr. International. He is also the winner of Kingfisher Model of the year 2005. In May 2014, he won the reality stunt show "". He made his Bollywood debut with Vikram Bhatt's super-hit horror thriller "1920". He is currently appearing in television series "Aarambh," which marks his television debut.
