Melarsomine (melaminylthioarsenate) is a trypanocidal agent. In the U.S., it is marketed under the trade names "Immiticide" (Merial) and "Diroban" (Zoetis), and is approved by the FDA's Center for Veterinary Medicine for the treatment of adult heartworm ("Dirofilaria immitis") infection in dogs. It is not approved for treatment in cats, or dogs in late-stage infection.
