Inteha (Hindi: इन्तेहा, Urdu: انتہا English: Limit ) is a Bollywood film released on 24 October 2003. It was produced by Mukesh Bhatt and directed by Vikram Bhatt, and stars Ashmit Patel, Vidya Malvade and Nauheed Cyrusi. It is inspired by the Hollywood film "Fear". It marked a debut film for Ashmit Patel. 3 months after the release of this movie, another movie directed by Vikram Bhatt titled "Aetbaar" was released and that too was based on the Hollywood film "Fear" .
