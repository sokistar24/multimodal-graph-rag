Usman Ally is an American film, stage and television actor. In 2015, Ally won an Obie Award for his role in "The Invisible Hand". He has appeared in several stage productions including "The Elaborate Entrance of Chad Deity", "The Jungle Book" and a production of "Around the World in 80 Days". He is known for his on screen roles such as Vincent on "Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D." and The hook-handed man in "A Series of Unfortunate Events".
