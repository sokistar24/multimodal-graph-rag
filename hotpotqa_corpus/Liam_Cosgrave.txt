William Michael "Liam" Cosgrave (born 13 April 1920) is a former Irish Fine Gael politician who served as Taoiseach from 1973 to 1977 and the Leader of the Fine Gael Party from 1965 to 1977. He served as Leader of the Opposition from 1965 to 1973, Minister for External Affairs from 1954 to 1957, Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for Industry and Commerce and Government Chief Whip from 1948 to 1951. He was a Teachta Dála (TD) from 1943 to 1981.
