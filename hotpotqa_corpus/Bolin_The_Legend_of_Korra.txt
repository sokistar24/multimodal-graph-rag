Bolin (愽林 , Bó Lín ) is a major fictional character in Nickelodeon's animated television series "The Legend of Korra", which aired from 2012 to 2014. The character and the series, a sequel to "", were created by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko. He is voiced by P. J. Byrne. Bolin is able to manipulate the classical element of earth, which is known as earthbending. It is revealed in the third season that he is also able to create and control lava, which is a very rare sub-ability called lavabending.
