Hormizd VI (Persian: ЩҮШұЩ…ШІвҖҺ вҖҺ ) was a Sasanian king of parts of Persia from 630 to 631. He was one of the many pretenders who rose after the murder of Khosrau II (590вҖ“628) in 628. He maintained himself about two years (630вҖ“632) in the district of Nisibis. He was the grandson of Khosrau II.
