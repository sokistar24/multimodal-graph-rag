Jessica Chobot (born Jessica Lynn Horn; July 7, 1977) is an American on-camera host and writer. She has hosted the IGN shows "IGN Strategize" and "Weekly Wood", which also runs on Xbox Live; she previously worked as presenter of the IGN Daily Fix. Since 2013 she is the host of Nerdist News and "Nerdist News Talks Back" for Nerdist Industries. In 2014 she launched her own podcast titled "Bizarre States".
