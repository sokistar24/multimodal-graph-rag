Nagaram Marupakkam (English: The other side of the town ) is a 2010 Indian Tamil action film co-produced written and directed by Sundar C., who himself plays the lead role. Co-produced by his wife Kushboo, the film also stars Anuya Bhagvath and Vadivelu in pivotal roles and features music by Thaman. Sundar's first directorial after five years, "Nagaram" released on 19 November 2010, it is based on the 1993 American movie Carlito's Way . The film is very well known for comedy sequences and portrayal of the comic character "Style Pandi" by Vadivelu.
