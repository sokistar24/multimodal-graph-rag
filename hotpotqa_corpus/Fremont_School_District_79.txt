Fremont School District 79 is a school district located in Mundelein, Illinois, and it is composed of Fremont Elementary School, Fremont Intermediate School, and Fremont Middle School. Fremont Middle School, the one in which the students of the most seniority attend, 16% feed into Adlai E. Stevenson High School in Lincolnshire, Illinois. Seventy-nine percent of the students feed into Mundelein High School in Mundelein, Illinois, and the other 5% of the students either will go to Grayslake Central High School in Grayslake, Illinois or Carmel Catholic High School in Mundelein, Illinois . The current superintendent is Dr. Bill Robertson, with the principals of Fremont Elementary School, Fremont Intermediate School, and Fremont Middle School being Stefan Ladenburger (both elementary and intermediate) and Pamela Motsenbocker.
