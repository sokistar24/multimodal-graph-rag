Kirton is a village and a civil parish in the shire county of Suffolk, England, situated off the A14 road, about 4.5 km from Felixstowe and around 10 km from Ipswich. The closest train station to Kirton is Trimley. According to the 2011 census, Kirton had a population of 1,146. It is located between the River Debben and the River Orwell. The village of Kirton covers a total area of 8.3 km2 . As the county of Suffolk lacks quarries, many of the buildings are made from flint, clay and timber.
