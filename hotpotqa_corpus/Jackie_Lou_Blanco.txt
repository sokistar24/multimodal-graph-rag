Jacqueline Lourdes Blanco-Davao (born February 11, 1964) is a Filipina actress and aerobic instructor of Filipino descent. During the 1980s and the 1990s, she appeared in different film genres including "Hihintayin Kita sa Langit", (1991), "Si Aida, Si Lorna, o Si Fe", (1989) "Misis mo, Misis ko", (1988) and "Palabra de honor" (1983).
