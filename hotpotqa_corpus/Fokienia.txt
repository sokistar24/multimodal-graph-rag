Fokienia is a genus of conifer tree belonging to the cypress family. In its characteristics, "Fokienia" is intermediate between the genera of "Chamaecyparis" and "Calocedrus". Genetically "Fokienia" is much closer to "Chamaecyparis", and not all researchers recognize "Fokienia" as a separate genus. The genus comprises only one living species, Fokienia hodginsii or Fujian cypress (; Vietnamese: "Pơmu" ), and one fossil species ("Fokienia ravenscragensis").
