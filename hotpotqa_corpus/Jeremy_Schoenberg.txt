Jeremy Schoenberg (born May 8, 1972), born in Los Angeles County, California, United States. Jeremy started out as a child star on both film and television. He played on "Days of Our Lives" from 1980 to 1982 as Johnny, Marlena's foster child. Jeremy was nominated for this role in the category of Best Young Actor - Daytime TV Series in the 2nd Annual Young Artist Awards. He also voiced Linus van Pelt on "Is This Goodbye, Charlie Brown? "
