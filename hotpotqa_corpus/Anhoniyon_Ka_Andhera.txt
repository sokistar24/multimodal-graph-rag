Anhoniyon Ka Andhera was an Indian television horror series that premiered on 26 February 2011 on Colors channel, and aired weekly on every Saturday at 11 PM IST. It ended on 9 July 2011. The series is produced by Bollywood film producer Vikram Bhatt, and each story of the show revolves around Anahita Malik, a girl who has supernatural powers. Vikram Bhatt film Haunted 3D stars Mahaakshay Chakraborty and Tia Bajpai also made their appearance on 30 April 2011 to promote their film.
